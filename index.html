Digidog Deathmatch is an Android game built using the Unity game engine. Customize your character's stats and skills, then battle online or offline in a 5 minute long action-combat deathmatch!

Controls

Tap the Attack Button to launch a melee attack, or hold and release to launch a strong melee attack which stuns the enemy for 1 second.
Melee attacks consume energy, identified in-game by the white bar underneath Player names.

Tap the Spell Button to cast your primary spell, or hold to cast your secondary spell.
Spell casting consumes mana, identified in-game by the blue bar underneath Player names.

Tap the Jump Button to jump.
Characters can double jump and jump off of walls.

Tap the Dodge Button to dodge all attacks for 1 second. Dodging breaks out of stuns/hitstops.
Depending on your dodge skills, holding and releasing the dodge button may adjust the functionality of your dodge.

Character Customization

Stats

Allocate 65 stat points across 5 stats:

Strength: determines maximum damage

Precision: determines minimum damage

Speed: determines movement speed

Stamina: reduces the mana/energy cost of spells/attacks by 1% per point

Crit: allows for a % chance to score a critical hit (150% damage) on any attack

Skills

Choose a dodge type and up to two additional skills that impact your dodge:

Roll: roll forward, ignoring all attacks for 1 second

  • Can take Roll Jump, causing roll to jump your character upwards
  • Can take Roll Bomb, allowing you to launch towards the ground after rolling, causing an explosion on impact that knocks back enemies

Dash: dash forward, ignoring all attacks for 1 second

  • Can take Charge, allowing the use of an improved dash which knocks back all enemies in your path
  • Can take Dash Distance +, increasing the total distance of dash by 50%

Spells

Choose a primary and secondary spell to aid in combat:

Primary Spells:
  • Fireball: launch a fireball that deals 300% damage
  • Spell Jump: spend 25 mana to jump upwards
  • Stat Roll: gain a 1-6 point increase in a random stat, up to three times per death
Secondary Spells:
  • Flamethrower: cast a flamethrower that burns enemies every 0.1 seconds for 25% damage
  • Levitate: spend 10 mana per second to slow your fall
  • Explosion: charge and release an explosion that deals up to 500% damage to all enemies around you

Gameplay Tutorial

  1. Go to the Character Customization menu to customize your stats and skills.
  2. After customizing your character, select either single player or multi player at the main menu. Single player will immediately start a game against NPCs, while multiplayer will search for active game queues with other players and attempt to join them.
  3. Upon entering a game, you have 5 minutes to gain as many points as possible (pictured at the top left of the game UI). Points are earned by doing damage and securing kills.
  4. Thats it! The player with the highest number of points at the end of the game wins.
  5. Digidog Deathmatch can be downloaded on Google Play.
